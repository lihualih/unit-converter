About Volume Conversion

Volume conversion is needed in cooking, chemistry, and everyday life. The metric system uses liters and milliliters, while the US customary system uses gallons, cups, and fluid ounces.

Volume Units

  • Liters (L): The SI unit of volume. Used worldwide.
  • Gallons (gal): Used in the US (US gallon = 3.785 L) and UK (imperial gallon = 4.546 L).
  • Milliliters (ml): 1/1000 of a liter. Used for small quantities.
  • Cups: Common in cooking. 1 US cup = 236.588 ml.
  • Fluid Ounces (fl oz): 1 US fl oz = 29.5735 ml.
