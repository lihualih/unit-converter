About Speed Conversion

Speed is the rate at which an object covers distance. Different units are used in different contexts — km/h and mph for driving, m/s for physics, and knots for aviation and maritime navigation.

Speed Units

  • km/h (kilometers per hour): Used worldwide for road speeds and weather.
  • mph (miles per hour): Used in the US, UK, and a few other countries for road speeds.
  • m/s (meters per second): The SI unit used in science and engineering.
  • knots: Used in aviation and maritime. 1 knot = 1 nautical mile per hour = 1.852 km/h.
