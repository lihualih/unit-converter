About Weight Conversion

Our weight converter supports both metric and imperial units of mass. The kilogram (kg) is the base unit of mass in the International System of Units (SI), while the pound (lb) is commonly used in the United States and United Kingdom.

Metric Units

The metric system uses the kilogram as the base unit. Grams (g) are used for lighter objects, while metric tons (t) are used for very heavy loads. 1 kilogram = 1000 grams = 0.001 metric tons.

Imperial Units

The imperial system uses pounds and ounces. 1 pound = 16 ounces. The stone (st) is used in the UK and Ireland for body weight: 1 stone = 14 pounds = 6.35 kg.
