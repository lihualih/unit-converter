About Area Conversion

Area conversion is essential for real estate, agriculture, construction, and land measurement. Different countries use different units, making conversion tools invaluable.

Area Units

  • Square Meters (m²): The SI unit of area, used worldwide.
  • Square Feet (ft²): Commonly used in the US and UK for real estate.
  • Acres: Used in the US and UK for land measurement. 1 acre = 43,560 ft².
  • Hectares: Used internationally for large land areas. 1 hectare = 10,000 m².
  • Square Kilometers: Used for very large areas like cities and countries.
