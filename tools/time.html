About Time Conversion

Time conversion is useful for project planning, scientific calculations, and everyday scheduling. Our converter uses standard time units.

Time Units

  • Second (s): The SI base unit of time.
  • Minute (min): 1 minute = 60 seconds.
  • Hour (hr): 1 hour = 60 minutes = 3,600 seconds.
  • Day: 1 day = 24 hours = 86,400 seconds.
  • Week: 1 week = 7 days = 168 hours.
  • Year: 1 year = 365 days = 8,760 hours (non-leap year).

Note

This converter uses standard approximations. A year is calculated as 365 days (31,536,000 seconds). Leap years (366 days) and the exact solar year (365.2425 days) are not accounted for. For precise astronomical calculations, use specialized tools.
