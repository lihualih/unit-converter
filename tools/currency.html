About Currency Conversion

Our currency converter provides real-time exchange rates for over 30 world currencies. Rates are fetched from the Open Exchange Rates API and updated every 30 minutes to ensure accuracy.

How to Use the Currency Converter

  1. Enter the amount you want to convert in the "Amount" field
  2. Select the source currency from the "From" dropdown
  3. Select the target currency from the "To" dropdown
  4. The converted amount appears instantly
  5. Use the swap button (⇄) to quickly reverse the conversion

Supported Currencies

We support major world currencies including USD (US Dollar), EUR (Euro), GBP (British Pound), JPY (Japanese Yen), CAD (Canadian Dollar), AUD (Australian Dollar), CHF (Swiss Franc), CNY (Chinese Yuan), INR (Indian Rupee), MXN (Mexican Peso), BRL (Brazilian Real), KRW (South Korean Won), and many more.

Exchange Rate Accuracy

Exchange rates are sourced from the Open Exchange Rates API (open.er-api.com), which aggregates data from multiple financial institutions. While our rates are highly accurate for informational purposes, they may differ slightly from rates offered by banks and currency exchange services, which typically include a markup.
