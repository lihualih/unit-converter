About Length Conversion

Our length converter supports both metric and imperial units, making it easy to convert between measurement systems used around the world.

Metric Units

The metric system uses meters as the base unit of length. Kilometers (km) are used for long distances, centimeters (cm) and millimeters (mm) for small measurements.

Imperial Units

The imperial system uses feet and inches in the United States and a few other countries. Miles are used for long distances, and yards are commonly used in sports and construction.

Common Conversions

  • Meters to Feet: 1 m = 3.28084 ft
  • Kilometers to Miles: 1 km = 0.621371 mi
  • Centimeters to Inches: 1 cm = 0.393701 in
  • Meters to Yards: 1 m = 1.09361 yd
